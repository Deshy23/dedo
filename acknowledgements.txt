`BGarment` dataset comes from  Berkeley Garment Library (http://graphics.berkeley.edu/resources/GarmentLibrary/) with minimal modifications:
Reorienting axis, centering of objects

`Sewing` dataset comes from Maria Korosteleva and Sung-Hee Lee.   Generating Datasets of 3D Garments with Sewing410Patterns. InConference on Neural Information Processing Systems, Datasets and Benchmarks411Track (Round 1), 2021
The garment objects in the dataset were simplified using Blender script from 10,000 to 200 ~ 2,000 vertices
